Lead Designer ¬∑ 2020

All Staff Video Game

Designer ¬∑ 2020

All Staff is an open source, top-down 2D RPG with a pixel-based 16x16 px art style. The game follows a noble knight on his quest to amass the most skilled UX Research and Design team to defeat the Evil Site once and for all. All Staff was developed on Unity and designed in pyxelEdit by Nneka Nnagbo and Elaine Wang.

See the complete project on Github or view the Process Documentation.
