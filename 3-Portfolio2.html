User Language ID Pilot Study — Piloting new tools to investigate user comprehnsion of tax terminology

November 2019

I worked alongside two analysts to identify the most frequent tax-related terms on Canada Revenue Agency's (CRA) Personal Income Tax (PIT) pages and built a survey to assess user comprehension of tax-related terminology.

We graded the accuracy of user-submitted definitions for each term. We performed a preliminary qualitative analysis of the user-submitted definitions to reveal insights and content recommendations, as well as broader agency content implicationsas.
