CONTEXT Background

I worked as the lead UX and Voice Interface Designer on a multidisciplinary team to solve a complex, business problem for the Government of Canada. For over 3 months, we conducted interviews, researched, prototyped, tested, and developed a conversational voice bot, powered through Amazon Lex, to help Canadians find the correct mailing address for their tax forms.
